HOW TO CITE

In November 2015 paper describing Microscopy Image Browser was accepted for publication in PLOS Biology, please cite it whenever referring to the software (the reference will be updated as soon as the article is published):

Microscopy Image Browser: A platform for segmentation and analysis of multidimensional datasets
I. Belevich, M. Joensuu, D. Kumar, H. Vihinen and E. Jokitalo
PLOS Biology, PLoS Biology 2016 Jan 4;14(1):e1002340. doi: 10.1371/journal.pbio.1002340

We appreciate if you let us know by email (ilya.belevich -=@=- helsinki.fi) when you cite MIB. This helps us to develop and maintain the program.
